Steakhouse Baked Potatoes

Description

Wondering how restaurants get those perfect potatoes? Hereâ€™s the hints on how to make the best oven baked potatoes with salty, crispy skin and fluffy potato inside.

Ingredients

  • 4 large Russet potatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Wash and dry the potatoes.
  3. Pierce the potato 2-3 times with a fork.
  4. Rub oil all over the potatoes (or choose one of the other options above to rub on the outside).
  5. Rub salt all over the potatoes. We prefer coarse sea salt or pink himalayan salt.
  6. Place the potatoes on a baking sheet and bake for about 45 minutes.
  7. The exact baking time will depend on how large the potatoes are. The potato should be tender inside if you poke a fork into it.
  8. Serve with butter, cheese, chives, sour cream and all of your favorite toppings!
